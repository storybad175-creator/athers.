PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 2025-12-18

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and Free Fire UID. We also collect transaction data when you use the wallet or watch ads.

We use your information to manage your account, facilitate tournament entries, verify match results, and provide customer support. Your email and phone number are used for critical communications like prize distribution.

3. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, but no online platform is 100% secure. You provide your data at your own risk.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for advertisement (AdSense) and Firebase for backend storage. These services may have their own privacy policies.
