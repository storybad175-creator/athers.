REFUND POLICY

NO REFUNDS UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES

Once a tournament entry fee is paid or Ad Coins are spent, they cannot be refunded.

1. Tournament Entry

Failure to join the match on time, disconnection, or disqualification due to rule violations (hacking, teaming, etc.) will result in forfeiture of the entry fee. No refunds will be issued.

2. Ad Coins

Ad Coins are non-refundable and non-transferable. They have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for real currency.

3. Deposits

Deposits made to the wallet are final. We do not support withdrawals ofdeposited funds except through winning tournament prizes.
